Soundtracks

Juan Perusin studied the drums long time ago and started working in different projects in Barcelona as a drummer and composer, he also worked teaching and as a session musician both for studio and live sessions. Since 2012 and to date he worked in Berlin, mostly with the piano, creating many different kind of soundtracks in his project "AmalgaMad". Composition, all instruments and mix by Juan Perusin. AmalgaMad is ready to collaborate with an open mind and looking for any kind of interesting projects that need a soundtrack. The ones that you can find in the link below are only some of them.

Check out also his work on Barnatic.
Barnatic (2011): Juan Perusin and Jeroni Castellá are Barnatic. These two musicians from Barcelona, after having worked for several years on the songs of each other and having worked together as session musicians in live concerts, decided to compose an album together and create a band composed primarily of these two guys. A duo that, however, sounds like a band. Jero with his Chapman Stick (a complex instrument that could be described as a bass and a guitar simultaneously) and vocals; and Juan on drums, piano, vocals, voice-over, lyrics and music production. The two using midi sounds occasionally. During the recording sessions, the duo featured some musicians and friends whom they expect also to collaborate on some of their concerts.The band made two kinds of concerts: The basic concert with the duo playing different elements according to the song and the big one with all the musicians who collaborated on the album and/or others. The album features: Guillermo Cides on stick-guitar, Chikiboom vocal trio, Luc Suarez on slide-guitar, Albert Miguez on Hammond and Clara Manjón on cello.
